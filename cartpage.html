Meesho Shipping & Delivery Charges

Meesho offers the lowest shipping cost across all online platforms in India. We have tied up with multiple Logistics

Shipping Timeline

The orders must be shipped within 2-3 days from the date of receiving the order within the agreed time provided
by you (also known as Service Level Agreement or SLA). You can check the status of your order and the days left for
dispatch on the Meesho Supplier Panel.

  • All return & RTO related charges can be tracked &

  • Please note that for some exceptional cases, the
